**The Go-To**

* **Usage Rate:** High usage rate  
* **Time of Possession:** High (primary ball-handler).  
* **Playmaking:** High Assist % and high Pick and Roll frequency.  
* **Shot Creation:** Majority of shots are unassisted.

## **The Perimeter Player**

* **Shot Profile:** High 3-Point Attempt Rate  
* **Low Usage:** Low usage  
* **Defensive Role:** Guards the opposing team's best perimeter players.  
* **Movement:** High distance traveled on defense.

## **The Rim Player**

* **Shot Selection:** Majority of shots taken within 0–3 feet of the rim.  
* **Efficiency:** Very high FG% (mostly dunks and layups).  
* **Rim Protection:** High Block % and Contested Shots.  
* **Rebounding:** High Offensive Rebound Rate.  
* **Height:** Taller.

## **The Stretch Big**

* **Floor Spacing:** High 3 Point Attempt Rate for their height.  
* **Effects:** Pulls opposing centers away from the paint.  
* **Post-Up:** Low frequency of traditional back to basket play.  
* **Mobility:** Generally lower defensive rebounding than Rim Players.

## **The Scorer**

* **Shot Location:** High frequency of mid range shots and tough iso jumpers.  
* **Usage:** Moderate-High.  
* **Shot Utility:** Can create a shot late in the shot clock.  
* **Low Playmaking:** Low Assist-to-Usage ratio.

## **The Smarts**

* **Efficiency of Play:** High Assist to Turnover ratio.  
* **Impact:** High Screen Assists and Hockey Assists.  
* **Intelligence:** Elite defensive positioning and deflections.  
* **Low Scoring:** Generally low PPG, impact is felt through plus/minus.

## **Downhill Attacker**

* **Rim Pressure:** High number of drives per game.  
* **Free Throws:** High Free Throw Attempt Rate.  
* **Transition:** Often leads the team in fast break points.  
* **Athleticism:** Heavily dependent on first-step explosion and downhill speed.
